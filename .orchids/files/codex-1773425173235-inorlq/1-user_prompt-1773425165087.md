Website Build Brief – "AI Beyond Intelligence" Conference Sponsored by Erste Bank

Concept & Aesthetic Direction

Build a premium, high-impact conference website that feels like it belongs in 2026 — not a generic event page. The primary sponsor is Erste Bank, so the design language should feel financial-sector trustworthy but bleeding-edge innovative. Think: the gravitas of a top-tier bank meeting the energy of a frontier tech conference.

Color palette:





Primary: deep navy #0A1628, midnight blue #0D2147



Accent: Erste Bank blue #1B4FD8, electric cyan #00C2FF



Surface: subtle dark glass #111D36 with frosted/glassmorphism panels



Text: near-white #E8EDF5, muted slate #8B9AB5



Gold accent #C9A84C — used sparingly for premium highlights only

Aesthetic: Luxury fintech meets research conference. Dark glassmorphism cards, sharp geometry, subtle grid overlays, animated gradient mesh background in deep blues. No purple. No generic gradients. Everything should feel precise and intentional.

Typography: Pair Syne or DM Serif Display for headings with IBM Plex Sans for body — gives a scientific/institutional weight. All imported from Google Fonts.

Animations: Staggered fade-in on scroll, floating particles or subtle animated grid in hero, smooth section transitions. Crisp, not flashy.
